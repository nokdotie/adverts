Description

Elegant Galway City Waterfront Apartment, 10 Tobar na mBan , Lough Atalia Road

This luxurious 2 bedroom apartment in Galway City boats sweeping waterfront views of Galway bay and state of the art amenities.

High ceilings with large floor to ceiling windows in both bedrooms and the open plan living room fill the space with natural light while the modern gas fireplace offers light and warmth from within.

Handsome bedrooms provide plush and tasteful comfort and adjoining marble-tiled bathrooms feature double basin sinks and a generous full Jacuzzi bath.

Expansive wood decked balconies showcase the stunning panoramic vista and provide protected al-fresco dining as well as outdoor access for both bedrooms.

Generous proportions and lavish decor combine with spectacular views in this prestigious residence to offer both comfort and security whilst being only a five minute stroll away to Eyre Square and all available amenities and enjoyments that Galway has to offer.
