Development Description

    Phase 1 Sold Out. Phase 2 and New Show Homes Coming Soon. This exceptional development is located directly opposite the Powerscourt Estate with incredible views of the Sugar Loaf, Wicklow Mountains and within walking distance of the picturesque village of Enniskerry Brought to you by Westin Homes / Hayfield Homes, Dargle Demesne consists of 27 substantial 3, 4 & 5 bedroom detached homes ranging from approximately 124sqm-343sqm. The homes have been meticulously designed with a high class finish to meet the demand of everyday modern living. Thought and care is evident throughout this development which further enclaves the attractiveness of these a-rated energy efficient homes. Classic handcrafted kitchens have been thoughtfully designed by Cawley furniture with Silestone worktops by Cosentino. Elegant handcrafted fitted wardrobes are included in all bedrooms by Cawley furniture. All bathrooms include a high-quality of sanitary ware and fittings by Villeroy and Boch. Each garden has been tastefully landscaped by Jane McCorkell and feature large stylish patio areas directly off the living area. Enniskerry has a vibrant community and is home to an array of independent boutiques, cafes, restaurants, hotels and spas including Avoca Handweavers Store, Espa @ The 5 Star Powerscourt Hotel and The Rainforest Spa @ Summerhill House Hotel. For further information please contact our New Homes team on 01 237 4500 / newhomes@knightfrank.ie
