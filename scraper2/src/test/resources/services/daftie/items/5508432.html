Development Description

    **NEW SHOWHOUSE IS NOW OPEN AND AVAILABLE FOR VIEWINGS** **THE JEWEL IN THE CROWN OF THESE HOMES ARE THE EXTRA-LARGE SOUTHWEST FACING TOP FLOOR TERRACE OVERLOOKING THE PARK AND THE DUBLIN MOUNTAINS** Last two houses remaining. Chandos Lane is an exclusive scheme of 6 completed luxurious family homes. These homes have been designed with great attention to detail resulting in properties of exceptional elegance and craftsmanship. Upon entering, the feature architecturally designed solid white oak slatted staircase, sets the tone for the workmanship and style of the home. The open plan ground floor is split level, and the living room truly has the wow factor with 10 ft ceilings and feature sliding doors to the southwest facing garden, complete with patio and lawn, perfect for entertaining. The first floor has two generous bedrooms complete with en-suite shower/ bathrooms. Each bedroom has custom fitted wardrobes. The layout of this floor could be reconfigured to allow for 3 double bedrooms if desired, with plans available on request. The master suite is located on the top floor; however, this is a versatile room which could be a fantastic second living room. There is an en-suite shower room and generous bespoke fitted wardrobes. The setting of Chandos Lane offers a sense of privacy and seclusion, with the benefit of being in a highly accessible location. Set back in a gated enclave, the properties are completely unobstructed to the rear, overlooking the leafy Dodder Park. Every conceivable convenience is located within walking distance, including shopping, restaurants, and caf s in the vibrant villages of Milltown, Ranelagh, Rathgar and Ballsbridge. The country s top schools on your doorstep, such as Alexandra College, Sandford Park, Mount Anville, Muckross, Our Lady s Grove Gonzaga College. UCD Belfield is within walking distance and Trinity College is easily accessible via Luas ride. The Milltown Luas stop is a short walk from the development and the M50 motorway is within easy reach.
