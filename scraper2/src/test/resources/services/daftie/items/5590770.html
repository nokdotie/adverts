Description

  • Sale Type: For Sale by Private Treaty
  • Overall Floor Area: 206 m²
Situated at the foothills of Mount Errigal, Campbells are delighted to have been favoured with instructions to present this beautiful five bedroom property for sale to the open market which is sure to appeal to many as the perfect family / holiday home / rental property. This well appointed five bedroom property is situated on an delightfully well maintained site extending to approximately 0.7 acres with splendid views across Dunlewey lake and only a short walk from the famed Poisoned Glen. Boasting frontage onto the R251 (Main Dunlewey - Termon Road) meaning the property is convenient to Dunlewey village and its amenities, Glenveagh National Park, The 4* An Chúirt Hotel and many other notable attractions whilst the major towns of Gweedore, Dungloe & Letterkenny are all within a 30 minutes drive from the property. Internally the property has been well maintained throughout comprising a generous sized living room with inbuilt solid fuel stove which is connected to the heating system as well as a recently installed wood pellet stove in the bright spacious kitchen / dining room, five airy double bedrooms (4 ensuite) as well as a fully tiled main bathroom and much more. Viewing comes highly recommend and by strict appointment only with Campbells Auctioneers.
