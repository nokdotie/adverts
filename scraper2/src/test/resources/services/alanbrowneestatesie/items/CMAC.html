Alan Browne of Alan Browne Estates is delighted to offer for sale a stunning site in a stunning location.

Opportunity to purchase a SERVICED SITE with FULL PLANNING PERMISSION for an A Rated detached house in the in the picture postcard Village of COURTMACSHERRY, CO. CORK.

Located only a short stroll from Courtmacsherry village yet situated on an extremely private setting.

Courtmacsherry is one of the most well known and popular villages in West Cork. It
has countless attractions and amenities including beautiful beaches, scenic nature walks, wildlife, horse riding, excellent fishing and of
course its pubs and welcoming locals. See also www.courtmacsherry.ie

All this and only a 45 minute drive from Cork city.
